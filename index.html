MY NAME IS BILLA

Billa is a 2009 Telugu action thriller starring Prabhas in a dual role as a ruthless international gangster and a simpleton lookalike. Directed by Meher Ramesh, it is a remake of the 1978 Hindi classic Don and the 2007 Tamil film of the same name. The movie is celebrated for its high production values and for showcasing Prabhas in an incredibly stylish and sophisticated avatar. Set primarily in Malaysia, the plot follows an undercover police mission to replace the deceased underworld don with his double. The film features a strong supporting cast, including Anushka Shetty and Namitha, who added glamour and depth to the high-stakes narrative.
