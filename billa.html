MY NAME IS BILLA

A Stylized Action Thriller

Movie Overview

Billa is a 2009 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Meher Ramesh. It is a remake of the 1978 Hindi classic Don and follows an undercover mission where a thief from Andhra Pradesh replaces a dreaded international gangster in Malaysia to infiltrate his mafia syndicate.
